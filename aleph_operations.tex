\section*{Core Binary Operations}

\begin{tabular}{ @{} l l l l @{}}
\toprule
\textbf{Operation} & \textbf{Syntax} & \textbf{Description} & \textbf{Primitive rule} \\
\midrule

Join   & $a \vee b$   & Least upper bound (LUB) & all: $\max$ \\
Meet   & $a \wedge b$ & Greatest lower bound (GLB) & all: $\min$ \\
Vav-cast & $a ::> b$ & Proof-carrying type cast & $d(a\otimes\text{\heb{ו}}, b) < \tau(\Omega)$ \\
Mediate & $\text{mediate}(w, a, b)$ & Triadic: $w \vee (a\otimes b)$ & join after tensor \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}

\subsection*{Tensor composition details}
Bottleneck primitives: $P$ (parity), $F$ (fidelity), $K$ (kinetic) -- the weaker partner wins.  
Union primitives: all others -- the stronger wins.  
Stoichiometry $S$: $n\!:\!m$ absorbs, $1\!:\!1$ only preserved with $1\!:\!1$.
